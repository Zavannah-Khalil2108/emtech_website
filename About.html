    ABOUT

    An intergalactic website that documents friends' recollections of their bonds. There are times in life when certain events become significant turning points in our development as people. One such important occasion is our graduation when friendships developed over years of common experiences come to an end with a sorrowful farewell.

    An air of melancholy coexists with enthusiasm and eagerness as graduation day draws near. The expectation of fresh starts offsets the reality that this chapter of our lives is coming to an end. The friends' range of emotions as they reminisce about their shared experiences and their journey together is well-captured on this site.

    In essence, this website serves as a tribute to the enduring friendship and the transformative journey of graduating friends. It is a reminder that while life may take us on different paths, the bonds we made along the way remain eternally present in this catalyst of memories.

Join us on a voyage through the universe, and let's create memories that will last beyond the stars!
